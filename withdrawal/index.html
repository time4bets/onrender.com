Deposits and Withdrawals Differ

A deposit method is not always the same as a withdrawal method. Processing time, verification and limits can change from one route to another.

  • Check minimum and maximum amounts
  • Read verification requirements
  • Keep bonus status in mind

Cashier Questions

Important payment notes are grouped near the end.

Why can a withdrawal take longer than a deposit?
Cashouts may include fraud checks, identity review or method processing time.
Can a bonus delay withdrawal?
Yes, if wagering or bonus conditions are still active.
Should I use someone else’s card?
No. Payment methods should belong to the account holder.
Are crypto payments always faster?
Not always. Network conditions and internal review can still affect timing.
Where should I check exact limits?
The cashier area should show the current limits and method details.
